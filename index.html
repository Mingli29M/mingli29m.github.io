About Me

Hi! I'm Mingli29, a passionate developer and content creator from China. I specialize in game development, Minecraft modding, and open-source software development. My journey in programming started with a love for gaming, which evolved into creating mods and tools that enhance the gaming experience for others.

I create Minecraft mods like BTDM and build creative tools like PixelStudio, an open-source pixel art application. I also produce content on Bilibili and Douyin, sharing tutorials, development updates, and gaming content with my community. I believe in the power of collaboration and love working with friends on exciting projects.

PixelStudio

PixelStudio is a full-featured, open-source pixel art application built with Godot 4.x. Designed as an alternative to Aseprite, it includes drawing tools, layer management, animation timeline, color palettes, and more!

Key Features:
  • Complete drawing tool suite
  • Layer system with blend modes
  • Frame-by-frame animation
  • Custom color palettes
  • Export to PNG, GIF, sprite sheets
